## Understanding Plans

Nexal AI offers different plans to fit your needs. Here's what each plan includes:

### Free Plan ($0/month)

**Perfect for**: Trying out the app, light usage

**Features:**
- 6 messages per day
- Access to free AI models only
- 2 images per day
- No video generation
- 1 custom persona
- No custom models
- Web & mobile access

**Best for**: Testing the app, occasional questions

### Plus Plan ($19.99/month or $79.99/year)

**Perfect for**: Regular users, students, professionals

**Features:**
- Unlimited messages
- Access to all AI models (100+ models)
- 1,000 premium model messages per day
- 100 images per day
- 10 videos per day
- Up to 200 custom personas
- Up to 200 custom models
- Full API access
- Web & mobile access

**Best for**: Daily use, content creation, learning, work

### Max Plan ($200/month)

**Perfect for**: Power users, businesses, heavy usage

**Features:**
- Unlimited messages
- Unlimited premium model messages
- Access to all AI models
- Unlimited images
- Unlimited videos
- Unlimited custom personas
- Unlimited custom models
- Full API access
- Advanced features
- Priority support

**Best for**: Professional use, businesses, maximum productivity

### Lifetime Plus ($99.99 one-time)

**Perfect for**: Long-term users who want to save money

**Features**: Same as Plus Plan, but pay once for lifetime access!

**Best for**: Users who plan to use the app long-term

## Daily Limits Explained

### What Are Daily Limits?

To ensure fair usage and quality service, some features have daily limits. These reset every day at midnight (your local time).

### Message Limits

- **Free**: 6 messages/day
- **Plus**: Unlimited (1,000 premium model messages/day)
- **Max**: Unlimited everything

### Image Limits

- **Free**: 2 images/day
- **Plus**: 100 images/day
- **Max**: Unlimited

### Video Limits

- **Free**: 0 videos/day (not available)
- **Plus**: 10 videos/day
- **Max**: Unlimited

### Premium Model Limits

- Premium models (like GPT-4) cost more to run
- Free plan: No access
- Plus plan: 1,000 messages/day with premium models
- Max plan: Unlimited

### What Happens When You Hit Your Limit

1. You'll see a friendly message explaining your limit
2. Options to:
   - Wait until midnight (limits reset)
   - Upgrade your plan
   - Use free models (if available)

### Checking Your Usage

**On Mobile:**
1. Go to Settings
2. Tap "Usage Statistics"
3. See:
   - Messages used today
   - Images generated today
   - Videos generated today
   - Days until limit reset

**On Web:**
1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Usage Statistics"
3. See:
   - Messages used today
   - Images generated today
   - Videos generated today
   - Days until limit reset

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Subscribe

**On Mobile (iOS):**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Browse available plans
3. Select a plan
4. Complete payment through App Store
5. Your plan activates immediately!

**On Mobile (Android):**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Browse available plans
3. Select a plan
4. Complete payment through Google Play
5. Your plan activates immediately!

**On Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Browse available plans
3. Select a plan
4. Complete payment through Stripe
5. Your plan activates immediately!

### Managing Subscriptions

#### Cancel Subscription

**iOS:**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Tap "Manage Subscription"
3. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
4. Cancel your subscription

**Android:**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Tap "Manage Subscription"
3. Go to Google Play → Subscriptions
4. Cancel your subscription

**Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Go to Account settings
4. Cancel your subscription

#### Change Plan

**On Mobile or Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Tap or click "Change Plan"
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm change
5. Prorated refund/charge applies

### Refund Policy

- Contact support for refund requests
- Refunds processed per platform policies
- Lifetime plans are non-refundable

## Understanding Premium Models

### What Are Premium Models?

Premium models are advanced AI systems that provide better results but require more computing resources. Examples include GPT-4, Claude Sonnet, and Gemini Pro.

### Free Models vs Premium Models

**Free Models:**
- GPT-3.5
- Claude Haiku
- Gemini Flash
- Fast responses
- Good for general tasks

**Premium Models:**
- GPT-4
- Claude Sonnet
- Gemini Pro
- Better quality
- More accurate
- Better for complex tasks

### When to Use Premium Models

- Complex reasoning tasks
- Detailed analysis
- Long-form writing
- Technical questions
- Document analysis
- Creative projects requiring high quality

### When Free Models Are Fine

- Quick questions
- Simple tasks
- General information
- Casual conversations
- Basic writing

<Tip>
Start with free models for simple tasks, and save your premium model messages for when you really need the extra quality and accuracy.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

### Do limits reset?

Yes, daily limits reset at midnight in your local timezone.

### What happens if I cancel?

You'll continue to have access to your plan until the end of your billing period. After that, you'll move to the Free plan.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund policies vary by platform. Contact support for assistance with refund requests.

### Do I lose my data if I downgrade?

No, your data is safe. You'll just have access to fewer features based on your new plan.

### Can I share my account?

Account sharing is not recommended and may violate our terms of service. Each user should have their own account.
