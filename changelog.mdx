## Version Information

Nexal AI maintains separate version numbers for mobile and web platforms:

- **Mobile**: Available on iOS and Android with version numbers shown in the app stores
- **Web**: Browser-based version with updates deployed automatically

Both platforms receive regular updates, but features may be released at different times. Check your platform's version to see what's available.
