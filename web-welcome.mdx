## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your browser-based AI assistant providing access to 100+ AI models, image generation, custom personas, and powerful productivity tools. No downloads requiredâ€”experience AI directly from your web browser.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Model Chat**: Access AI models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, xAI, and more
- **Image Generation**: Create stunning images from text descriptions
- **Custom Personas**: Build specialized AI assistants for your needs
- **Tools**: Generate documents, save memories, search the web
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Navigate efficiently with powerful shortcuts
- **Thinking Mode**: See the AI's reasoning process step-by-step
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access your conversations from any device
