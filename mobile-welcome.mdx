## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your mobile AI assistant bringing the power of artificial intelligence to your smartphone. Access over 100 AI models, generate images, create custom personas, and build mini-apps - all from your iOS or Android device.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Model Chat**: Access AI models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, xAI, and more
- **Image Generation**: Create stunning images from text descriptions
- **Voice Conversations**: Talk to AI hands-free
- **Custom Personas**: Build specialized AI assistants
- **Apps Builder**: Create custom mini-apps by chatting with AI
- **Tools**: Generate documents, save memories, set reminders, search the web
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access your conversations from any device
