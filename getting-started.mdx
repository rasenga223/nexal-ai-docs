## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your all-in-one AI assistant that brings the power of artificial intelligence to your mobile device and web browser. Access over 100 AI models, generate images, create custom personas, and more - all from one platform.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Model Chat**: Access AI models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, xAI, and more
- **Image Generation**: Create stunning images from text descriptions
- **Custom Personas**: Build specialized AI assistants for your needs
- **Voice Conversations**: Talk to AI hands-free on mobile
- **Tools**: Generate documents, save memories, set reminders, and more
- **Apps Builder** (Mobile): Create custom mini-apps with visual builder
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on iOS, Android, and web

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

Sign up using your email, Google account, or Apple ID. It's free to get started!

<a href="https://nexalai.com/signup" target="_blank">
  <Button>Create Account</Button>
</a>

### 2. Download the App

**iOS**: Download from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/nexalai)

**Android**: Download from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexalai)

**Web**: Visit [nexalai.com](https://nexalai.com)

### 3. Start Chatting

Once you're signed in, you're ready to go! Type a message and start your first conversation.
