## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your all-in-one AI assistant that brings the power of artificial intelligence to your mobile device and web browser. Think of it as having multiple AI experts ready to help you with:

- **Conversations**: Chat with over 100 different AI models
- **Creative Projects**: Generate images and videos from text descriptions
- **Document Analysis**: Upload and analyze PDFs, Word documents, and websites
- **Math Problems**: Get step-by-step solutions to complex math problems
- **Custom AI Assistants**: Create your own specialized AI helpers

## Quick Start Guide (Your First 5 Minutes)

### 1. Download and Install

**Mobile Apps:**
- Get Nexal AI from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
- Install the app on your device

**Web App:**
- Visit [nexalai.com](https://nexalai.com) in your browser
- No installation needed - works on any modern browser

### 2. Create Your Account

Open the app (mobile or web) and tap or click "Sign Up". You can choose to sign up with:

- **Email and password**: Enter your email and create a secure password (at least 8 characters)
- **Google account**: One-tap sign-in with your Google account
- **Apple ID**: Sign in with Apple (iOS and web)

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** Google Sign-in or Apple Sign-in provide the quickest setup and enhanced security.
</Tip>

### 3. Explore the Main Screen

The app is organized into main sections:

**Mobile App Tabs:**
- **Chat**: Talk with AI assistants
- **Generate**: Create images and videos
- **News**: Read AI-summarized news
- **Media**: View your generated images and videos
- **Settings**: Manage your account and preferences

**Web App Navigation:**
- **Chat**: Your main workspace for conversations
- **Generate**: Create images and videos
- **Media**: View your creations
- **Settings**: Account and preferences

### 4. Start Your First Chat

1. Open the Chat tab (mobile) or Chat section (web)
2. Type a question like "What is artificial intelligence?"
3. Tap Send (mobile) or press Enter (web)
4. Watch as the AI responds in real-time!

### 5. Try Image Generation

1. Open the Generate tab or section
2. Type a description like "a sunset over mountains"
3. Tap or click Generate
4. Wait a few seconds for your image to appear

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Options

**Email & Password:**
- Enter your email address
- Create a secure password (at least 8 characters)
- Verify your email address (check your inbox)
- You're ready to go!

**Google Sign-In:**
- Tap or click "Sign in with Google"
- Select your Google account
- Grant permissions
- You're automatically signed in!

**Apple Sign-In** (iOS and web):
- Tap or click "Sign in with Apple"
- Use Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
- You're signed in instantly!

### Account Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Tap or click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Create a new password
5. Sign in with your new password

## Understanding the App Layout

### Mobile App Layout

The mobile app is organized into 5 main sections, accessible via tabs at the bottom of your screen:

**Chat Tab** (Center):
- Your main workspace for conversations
- Start new chats or continue existing ones
- Access all AI models and personas
- Upload documents and images

**Generate Tab:**
- Create AI-generated images
- Generate videos from text
- Access your generation history

**News Tab:**
- Browse AI-summarized news articles
- Read articles optimized for mobile
- Stay updated on current events

**Media Tab:**
- View all your generated images
- Watch your generated videos
- Organize and share your creations

**Settings Tab:**
- Manage your subscription
- Adjust app preferences
- View your usage statistics
- Access help and support

### Web App Layout

The web app provides a similar experience with navigation optimized for desktop:

- **Sidebar Navigation**: Quick access to all features
- **Chat Interface**: Full-screen conversation area
- **Generate Panel**: Dedicated space for image and video creation
- **Media Gallery**: Grid view of all your creations
- **Settings Dashboard**: Comprehensive account management

<Tip>
Both mobile and web apps sync your data automatically, so you can switch between devices seamlessly!
</Tip>
