## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your all-in-one AI assistant that brings the power of artificial intelligence to your devices. Access over 100 AI models, generate images, create custom personas, and more - all from one platform.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Model Chat**: Access AI models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, xAI, and more
- **Image Generation**: Create stunning images from text descriptions
- **Custom Personas**: Build specialized AI assistants for your needs
- **Voice Conversations**: Talk to AI hands-free on mobile
- **Tools**: Generate documents, save memories, set reminders, and more
- **Apps Builder** (Mobile): Create custom mini-apps by chatting with AI
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on iOS, Android (coming back soon), and web
