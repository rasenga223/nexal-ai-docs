Stay productive on the go by organizing your AI interactions into folders. The mobile app provides a seamless way to manage your projects and files from anywhere.

## Why Use Folders?

Folders on mobile help you maintain a clean workspace by grouping related items together. Whether you're researching, planning, or organizing documents, folders keep everything in one place.

### Mobile Features

- **Project Workspaces**: Group chats and files by topic for easier navigation.
- **Document Management**: Upload photos or documents directly from your phone.
- **AI Context**: Chatting inside a folder gives the AI access to the files stored in that folder.
- **Quick Actions**: Easy upload and deletion of project assets.

## Managing Folders on Mobile

### Creating & Managing

1. Access the **Folders** tab from the main navigation.
2. Tap the **Create Folder** icon.
3. Give your folder a name and description.
4. You can **Edit** or **Delete** folders by tapping the options menu next to the folder name.

## Adding Content

### Organizing Conversations

Keep your chat list organized by moving conversations into specific folders.

1. Long-press a conversation or tap the menu icon.
2. Select **Move to Folder**.
3. Choose the target folder.

### Uploading Files

You can add documents and images to your folders directly from your mobile device.

1. Open your folder in the **Folders** tab.
2. Tap the **Upload** button.
3. Choose to upload a **Document** or a **Photo** from your library.
4. All uploaded files are instantly available for the AI to reference.

## Folder Context in Chat

When you are in a folder-based conversation:

1. The AI is automatically aware of the **Folder ID**.
2. It can use tools to **search your knowledge base** specifically within that folder.
3. It can retrieve and analyze the specific files you've uploaded to that project.
