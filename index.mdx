## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI is your all-in-one AI assistant that brings the power of artificial intelligence to your mobile device and web browser. Whether you're chatting with AI, creating images and videos, analyzing documents, or building custom AI assistants, Nexal AI has everything you need.
