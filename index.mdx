## What is Nexal AI?

Nexal AI gives you access to over 100 AI models from leading providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, and xAI. Chat with AI, generate images, create custom personas, and extend capabilities with powerful tools - all in one app available on iOS, Android, and web.

### Key Features

- **100+ AI Models**: Chat with GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Llama, and more
- **Image Generation**: Create stunning images from text descriptions
- **Custom Personas**: Build specialized AI assistants for your needs
- **Voice Conversations**: Talk to AI hands-free on mobile
- **Tools**: Generate documents, save memories, search the web, and more
- **Apps Builder** (Mobile): Create custom mini-apps by chatting with AI
