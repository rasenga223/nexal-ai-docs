## Getting Started with Image Generation

### What is AI Image Generation?

Describe what you want to see, and AI creates it! No drawing skills needed - just describe your vision in words, and watch it come to life.

### How to Generate Your First Image

**On Mobile:**
1. Open the Generate tab
2. Make sure you're on the Image section
3. Tap the text input box
4. Describe what you want (e.g., "a futuristic city at sunset")
5. Choose image size and aspect ratio
6. Select a style preset (optional)
7. Tap Generate
8. Wait for your image to appear

**On Web:**
1. Open the Generate section
2. Click in the prompt input
3. Describe what you want (e.g., "a futuristic city at sunset")
4. Choose image size and aspect ratio
5. Select a style preset (optional)
6. Click Generate
7. Wait for your image to appear

### Understanding Aspect Ratios

Choose the right aspect ratio for your use case:

- **Square (1:1)**: Perfect for social media posts, profile pictures
- **Wide (16:9)**: Great for wallpapers, banners, presentations
- **Tall (9:16)**: Ideal for mobile wallpapers, stories, vertical content
- **Standard (4:3)**: Classic photo ratio, versatile for many uses
- **Portrait (3:4)**: Good for portraits and vertical compositions

### Output Formats

**Available Formats:**
- **PNG**: High quality, supports transparency
- **JPEG**: Smaller file size, good for photos
- **WebP**: Modern format, excellent compression

## Image Generation Tips

### Writing Effective Prompts

The better you describe what you want, the better the result!

**Good Prompt Examples:**
- "A serene mountain landscape at sunrise, with snow-capped peaks, a calm lake in the foreground reflecting the mountains, soft pink and orange sky, photorealistic style"
- "A cute cartoon robot with big eyes, holding a flower, pastel colors, simple background, friendly expression"

### Prompt Structure

1. **Subject**: What is the main focus? (e.g., "a cat")
2. **Details**: Add specifics (e.g., "orange tabby cat, sitting")
3. **Setting**: Where is it? (e.g., "on a windowsill")
4. **Style**: How should it look? (e.g., "photorealistic, soft lighting")
5. **Mood**: What feeling? (e.g., "peaceful, warm")

- **Too vague**: "a picture" â†’ Better: "a picture of a sunset over mountains"
- **Too many conflicting ideas**: Keep it focused
- **Forgetting style**: Always specify realistic, artistic, cartoon, etc.

### Style Presets

Style presets help you get consistent results:

**Available Styles:**
- **Realistic**: Photorealistic images
- **Artistic**: Paintings and artistic styles
- **Anime**: Anime and manga style
- **3D Render**: 3D rendered graphics
- **Sketch**: Hand-drawn sketch style
- **Digital Art**: Modern digital artwork

*And more...*

**How to Use:**
1. Tap or click "Style" before generating
2. Select a preset
3. Your prompt automatically includes style instructions
4. Generate your image

## In-Chat Image Generation

Generate images directly within your conversations:

**On Mobile:**
1. In any chat, type `/image` or ask the AI to create an image
2. Describe what you want
3. The AI generates the image in the conversation
4. View, save, or share directly from chat

**On Web:**
1. Ask the AI to create an image in your conversation
2. Describe what you want
3. The image appears in your chat
4. Click to view full size, download, or regenerate

## Managing Your Images

### Image Gallery

All your generated images are saved to your gallery:

**On Mobile:**
1. Go to the Media tab
2. View all your generated images
3. Tap to view full size
4. Share, download, or delete

**On Web:**
1. Access Gallery from the sidebar
2. Browse all generated images
3. Click to view, download, or delete

### Sharing Images

**On Mobile:**
- Tap on any image in the gallery
- Tap the share button
- Choose your sharing method

**On Web:**
- Click on any image
- Right-click to save or copy
- Use the share options if available
