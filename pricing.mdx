## FAQ

### Which plan should I choose?

- **Free**: Great for trying out the platform and occasional use
- **Lite**: Good for light daily use with some premium model access
- **Plus**: Best value for regular users who need full access
- **Pro**: For power users who need higher limits and priority support
- **Max**: For professionals who need unlimited access to everything

### Can I change plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

### What payment methods are accepted?

- **iOS**: Apple App Store (credit card, Apple Pay)
- **Android**: Google Play Store (credit card, Google Pay)
- **Web**: Credit card, debit card
