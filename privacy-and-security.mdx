## Your Data & Privacy

### What Data Is Stored

**We Store:**
- Your account information (email, name)
- Your conversations (so you can access them later)
- Your generated images and videos
- Your custom models and personas
- Usage statistics

**We Don't Store:**
- Your payment information (handled by App Store/Google Play/Stripe)
- Your device information beyond what's necessary
- Your location data
- Any data you don't explicitly provide

### How Conversations Are Saved

- Conversations are saved to your account
- Accessible from any device when signed in
- Stored securely on our servers
- Encrypted in transit and at rest

### Data Deletion

#### Delete Individual Conversations

**On Mobile:**
1. Long-press on conversation
2. Tap "Delete"
3. Confirmed deleted

**On Web:**
1. Right-click on conversation
2. Select "Delete"
3. Confirmed deleted

#### Delete All Data

**On Mobile:**
1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Tap "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion
4. All your data is permanently deleted

**On Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Click "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion
4. All your data is permanently deleted

### Export Your Data

Want a copy of your data?

**On Mobile:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Tap "Export My Data"
3. Request export
4. Receive email with download link
5. Download your data (usually within 24 hours)

**On Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Click "Export My Data"
3. Request export
4. Receive email with download link
5. Download your data (usually within 24 hours)

## Security Features

### Secure Authentication

- **Password Security**: Passwords are hashed (never stored in plain text)
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Available for extra security (coming soon)
- **OAuth Sign-In**: Google and Apple sign-in use secure protocols
- **Session Management**: Automatic logout after inactivity

### API Key Safety

- API keys are stored securely server-side
- Never exposed to client apps
- Encrypted at rest
- Access controlled and logged

### Best Practices

**For Your Account:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your account
- Sign out on shared devices
- Enable two-factor authentication when available

**For Your Data:**
- Don't share sensitive information in public models
- Review privacy settings regularly
- Delete data you no longer need
- Export data periodically for backup

**For Your Conversations:**
- Be mindful of what you share
- Don't include passwords or sensitive info
- Use private models for confidential data
- Delete conversations when done

## Privacy Settings

### Control What's Shared

**On Mobile:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Adjust settings:
   - Share usage data (helps improve app)
   - Share crash reports (helps fix bugs)
   - Marketing emails (opt in/out)

**On Web:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Adjust settings:
   - Share usage data (helps improve app)
   - Share crash reports (helps fix bugs)
   - Marketing emails (opt in/out)

### Data Retention

- Conversations: Kept until you delete them
- Generated content: Kept until you delete it
- Account data: Kept while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days

## Document Privacy

### How Documents Are Handled

- Documents are processed securely
- Files are encrypted in transit and at rest
- Your documents are only used to answer your questions
- Documents are not shared with other users

### Custom Model Privacy

**Private Models:**
- Only you can access them
- Your documents stay private
- Perfect for sensitive information

**Public Models:**
- Others can ask questions
- They cannot see your original documents
- They only see answers based on your knowledge base

<Tip>
For sensitive documents, always use private custom models. Never make models containing confidential information public.
</Tip>
