## What are Mini-Apps?

Mini-apps are lightweight, custom applications you can build directly in Nexal AI. Create tools, calculators, forms, and interactive experiences without writing code.

<Note>
**Availability**: Mobile only (iOS and Android)
</Note>

## How to Access the Apps Builder

**On Mobile:**
1. Open the Nexal AI app
2. Tap the Apps tab in the bottom navigation
3. Browse existing mini-apps or create your own
4. Tap "Create New App" to start building

## Creating Your First Mini-App

### Using the Visual Builder

1. **Start a New App**
   - Tap "Create New App"
   - Choose a template or start from scratch
   - Give your app a name and description

2. **Design Your Interface**
   - Drag and drop components
   - Add buttons, text fields, images, and more
   - Customize colors, fonts, and layout
   - Preview in real-time

3. **Add Functionality**
   - Connect components to actions
   - Set up data inputs and outputs
   - Configure logic and behavior
   - Test your app as you build

4. **Publish Your App**
   - Review your app
   - Tap "Publish" when ready
   - Your app appears in your Apps list
   - Share with others if desired

## Tips for Building Apps

**Start Simple**: Begin with basic functionality and add features gradually

**Test Often**: Preview your app frequently to catch issues early

**Use Templates**: Start with a template and customize it to save time

**Plan Ahead**: Sketch out your app's flow before building

<Tip>
The Apps feature is great for creating custom tools that you use frequently. Build once, use forever!
</Tip>
